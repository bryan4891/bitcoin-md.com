Understanding Bitcoin

At Atlas Urology, we believe financial integrity matters in healthcare — and Bitcoin is part of that philosophy.

Our Philosophy

Transforming Healthcare Finance

At Atlas Urology, we believe that a broken monetary system contributes to perverse incentives, posing significant challenges to the U.S. healthcare system. By embracing Bitcoin, we aim to mitigate these issues by securing our reserves on the Bitcoin network.

This decision reflects our commitment to financial integrity and stability — ensuring our ability to provide quality care without compromise.
