Meet the Team

Our Providers

Experienced, compassionate urological specialists dedicated to your care.

Urologist — MD

Dr. Bryan Allen

University of North Florida (BS) · University of South Florida (MD) · Alpha Omega Alpha Honor Society

Dr. Allen is a distinguished urologist specializing in prostate cancer, kidney cancer, bladder cancer, testicular cancer, endoscopic management of urinary stone disease, stress incontinence, BPH, erectile dysfunction, and low testosterone. He is an active member of the Florida Medical Association, Manatee Medical Society, and the American Urological Association. Outside of medicine, Dr. Allen is married to Dr. Rosabelle Campos, a practicing OB/GYN, and together they enjoy time with their three children.

Physician Associate — PA-C

Marybeth Shields

Florida State University (BS) · FSU College of Medicine (MS, PA-C) · Pi Alpha Honor Society

Marybeth is a board-certified Physician Associate with expertise in kidney stone disease, urologic oncology, voiding dysfunction, overactive bladder, incontinence, pelvic pain syndromes, urinary obstruction, prostatic enlargement, sexual dysfunction, ED, and urinary tract infections. Outside the office she enjoys cheering on the FSU Seminoles and spending time with her husband and their two golden retrievers, Ollie & Charlie.

Physician Associate — PA-C

Paige Carruthers

University of South Florida (BS, Biomedical Sciences & Public Health) · Nova Southeastern University (MMS, PA Studies, Honors)

Paige began her clinical career as an EMT in upstate New York before pursuing her passion for medicine at USF and Nova Southeastern University. She graduated with honors with her Master of Medical Science in Physician Assistant Studies. In her free time, she loves traveling with her husband Frank, spending time with her dogs, and baking.
